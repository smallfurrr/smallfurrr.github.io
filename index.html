minute.

Daily diary app with mood analysis
minute.

Created using HTML, CSS, React, Express.js, Node.js and PostgreSQL.

I was inspired to create this app as I feel there is still a societal stigma surrounding mental health issues in Singapore. People are not fully comfortable discussing topics such as therapy and medication, and tend to bottle up their emotions. I created this app to encourage people to perform a minute of self-reflection each day. The app then generates a dynamic Mood Report based on their entries and provides listening recommendations accordingly.
